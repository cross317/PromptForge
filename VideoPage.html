How to use PromptLight

Prompt light is a web site where you can take AI prompt for a full experience with AI.
First: if you are in the home page you can both tap "Start now" or "Free propts" in the menu(top-right of the screen)
Second: when you are finally in the free prompts section you can take some of the prompts you want just by copying them!
Third: there are different categories of prompts so you have to look for the one that you are intrested it and then tap on it.
Fourth: afgter you've copied the prompt you need you can go to your favourite AI and just paste it and you will have an accurate response.
Fifth: the last page is the one where you can give a feedback, if you'd like to change something just type your ideas there and send what you've written.
If you are not in the home page, if you want to go to the home page you will have to click on the "home" button.
